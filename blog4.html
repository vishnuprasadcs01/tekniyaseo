Fatura ERP: An E-invoicing Solution Certified by ZATCA for Secure & Seamless Business Operations in Saudi Arabia.

Fatura ERP is an E-invoicing solution provider in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia officially approved by ZATCA. Our goal is to establish a competitive market in information technology by providing complete IT solutions. Our first step in this venture was establishing an E-invoice solution for small and medium-level companies by providing QR code-generated invoice solutions.

With Fatura’s user-friendly design, managing your invoicing will be effortless. What makes us stand out in this field is the extensive support we provide to our customers. We assure you of a transparent cloud experience where your data will be secured and managed with the utmost discretion. Our technical team is highly talented and is dedicated to answering all the queries. The system is built to have customizations upon customer requirements.

Why Choose Fatura ERP for Your Business?

1. Authorized by ZATCA

Understanding the nuances of legal systems, the Fatura e-invoicing solution is designed to go well with the GAZT VAT solutions, according to the regulations of ZATCA in Saudi. The seamless and transparent solution will help your business become secure and systematize your data. As a ZATCA/GAZT-compliant e-invoicing software, you can enjoy an error-free and secure invoicing process in real time!

2. Streamlined

The e-invoicing system is technologically advanced, which helps your business save a lot of time required in involves manual invoicing. Electronic invoicing on a digital platform eliminates the need to manually enter data and file invoices. It also facilitates the storage of previous payment records and provides access to historical data at any time.

3. Accuracy

Because e-invoices can be sent instantly and directly to the customer, the risk of misdelivery or loss is significantly reduced. The integrated digital paper trail enables efficient tracking of invoice progress and improves communication between all parties involved. In addition, the data is collected and analyzed by the automated system, which makes the accounting process more reliable.

4. Comprehensible

A robust e-invoicing system works in tandem with your other accounts payable and supply chain tools and technologies. This allows full automation to be achieved so that existing customers do not have to repeatedly create invoices manually. Businesses find it easier to sort and track their records and often even find that their payments come in much faster. Our dedicated support team makes it more user-friendly and convenient for users.

5. Exclusive Assistance

Fatura has a dedicated team of engineers and support assistants to grapple with any challenges faced by customers. Unlike our competitors, we never neglect even minor queries from our customers. Our customer support team is keen to address all your e-invoicing issues.

6. API Integration

While opting for e-invoicing, data transactions and communication are the primary concerns of many businesses. One does not need to worry about it when choosing Fatura. The API integration feature makes data transactions and communication processes smoother. Hence, data transfer and communication are just a walk in the park when you choose Fatura as your e-invoicing partner.
