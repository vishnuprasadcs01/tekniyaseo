You can reach Tekniya through the contact form on our website, by email, or by phone. Our offices are based in Saudi Arabia and additional support in the UAE, Bahrain, and India. Inquiries are typically answered within one business day with a scheduled initial consultation.

A Tekniya consultant contacts you within one business day to schedule an initial consultation. We review your business requirements, map them against the service that fits best, and send a scoped proposal with timeline and pricing within 3 to 5 business days. Once you approve the proposal, the project team begins discovery and delivery on the agreed schedule.

The Aramco CCC certification process typically takes 8 to 16 weeks depending on your current cybersecurity maturity and the size of your IT infrastructure. The timeline includes initial gap assessment, documentation preparation, control implementation, and the final audit.

Yes. ZATCA Phase 2 (Fatoorah) e-invoicing applies to every VAT-registered business in Saudi Arabia. The rollout has been phased over the last two years by annual revenue, and the threshold drops each cycle, which pulls more businesses in. Setup means your invoicing system has to talk to ZATCA's platform through an approved e-invoicing solution. That platform generates the XML, the QR codes, and the cryptographic stamps for every invoice, automatically.

Cybersecurity is about keeping your systems, data, and people out of trouble. In Saudi Arabia specifically, it is also about compliance. Aramco runs its own CCC certification for supply chain vendors. We work across all three frameworks. The services run from endpoint protection and email security to firewall management, plus the audit-ready compliance documentation that most companies underestimate.

Odoo is open-source ERP software. It does what you would expect (accounting, inventory, CRM, HR).Tekniya is the official partner of Oodo ERP. We handle deployment, customization, and the ongoing support most companies don't realize they will need until six months after go-live.

On the digital marketing side, we run SEO and Google Ads, social media across Instagram, Snapchat, TikTok, and LinkedIn. email automation, branding, and monthly performance reports. It depends on where the business is in its growth.
