Tekniya- A Bridge between Your Ideas and Execution

Founded in 2019, Tekniya is a Saudi-based digital solutions company, with delivery teams across Saudi Arabia and a wider footprint in the UAE, Bahrain, and India. We work with businesses that need a single accountable partner for cybersecurity, software development, ERP implementation, network solutions, Microsoft 365, and digital marketing, rather than juggling separate vendors for each. Every engagement is scoped against measurable business outcomes, not activity reports.

Our Vision

By learning and integrating the waves of technology, our vision is to be the tech-savvy company that makes the strongest foundations for a techy world

Our Mission

To deliver technology and marketing solutions that solve real business problems, comply with Saudi regulatory standards, and scale with our clients as their needs evolve.

Why Choose Tekniya

Excellent Client Service

Clients are at the center of how Tekniya works. Our dedicated client service team handles every concern from project kickoff to delivery, whether your engagement involves cybersecurity, Aramco CCC certification, Odoo ERP, mobile app development, or digital marketing across Saudi Arabia.

We don't limit our thirst for new knowledge. Our team holds monthly training sessions covering the latest in cybersecurity, Microsoft 365, Odoo ERP, network infrastructure, ZATCA e-invoicing, and digital marketing, so we're always ready to bring current and compliant solutions to our clients.

Wide Range of Services

Our services encompass all the elements your business needs. From cybersecurity and Aramco CCC certification to network infrastructure, web and mobile app development, Odoo ERP, Microsoft 365 solutions, and digital marketing.
